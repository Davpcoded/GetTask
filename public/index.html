Task Management

Utilize our free open-source task management tool for all of your project management needs. Users can navigate to the Tasks page to get started and create a new task. The new task will then be displayed on the Tasks page the next time that the user navigates there. Each task will automatically record who it was created by, but it can also be assigned to either that same user or another to be completed. Users are able to view tasks, both past and present, and will be visually notified on the task list when the task is past it's completion date.

Invite Your Team

Our solution utilizes user authentication, allowing multiple users to each have their own login credentials and permissioned controls. We designed the system so that a team or group of users can all track their tasks on the platform simultaneously. Team members can see when a task is assigned in real time, preventing them from duplicating effort on the same task. Users can also un-assign tasks so that it can be passed over to another team member. When the ticket is un-assigned, the user that first started the task will provide an update detailing the work that was completed.

Get Competitive

Get competitive with GetTask! We know the tasks won't always be fun, but maybe at least tracking them can be. Our leaderboard shows users how they compare to their peers, gamifying the task management experience and differentiating the platform from industry competitors. Users who top the leaderboards will be rewarded with badges for their achievements. The badges will be tied to the users account and will display on the user list view, allowing team members to establish credibility as a top performer.
